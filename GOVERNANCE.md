# Governance

## Project Maintainer

This project is maintained by the original author(s).
The maintainer has final decision authority on:

- Scope definition
- Compliance wording
- Feature inclusion

---

## Decision Making

- Technical discussions are open via GitHub Issues
- Final decisions are made by the maintainer
- Compliance and legal safety take precedence over feature requests

---

## Contributions

- Contributions are welcome
- All contributions must comply with the project's compliance principles
- The maintainer may reject contributions that introduce legal or ethical risk

## Derivative Works

Derivative projects are welcome under the license terms.
However, projects that substantially replicate the structure,
terminology, or compliance model should clearly acknowledge
this repository as the original source.
